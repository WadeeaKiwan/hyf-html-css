The first opening

Computer Technology Center CTC was founded in 2006. It was basically an internet shop. In addition to sell accessories of computers, laptops and printers. Besides there was assembly for new PC’s and maintenance for computers and laptops.


The second opening

In 2010 was the second opening of CTC. It was not more internet shop. In addition to the earlier maintenance was also printers, routers and switches maintenance (software and hardware). There was also a wider variety of accessories of computers and laptops.

Besides selling different sorts of CD’s and DVD’s such as operating systems, variant programs, educational DVD’s and games.

In addition to that CTC had also design with the help of Adobe programs, Corel Draw or others design programs. Designing was available for posters, business cards and many more.

It was also available to make projects for companies, organizations or centers as a network design and implementation and servers and domains assembling and building, in other words the whole infrastructure and software.


Activities

CTC was also a center for doing activities and playing games with friends. It was also a very social place where all the related people had always gathered.


Destruction

We were obliged to leave Al Yarmouk Camp where CTC and our houses were in the end of 2012, because of the war which has begun after the revolution in Syria had broken out. We left our lovely and social center, that was full of happiness and colorful life for the family, the friends and the district self.


Last word

CTC is still the beautiful dream to assure the determination of life. It will be back some day and will be rebuild not only in the place where it was founded but also overseas.
